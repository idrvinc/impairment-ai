Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ImpairmentAI

How accurate is ImpairmentAI's detection system?

ImpairmentAI achieves 99.9% accuracy in detecting impairment through our advanced AI algorithms. Our system has been tested across diverse populations and conditions, ensuring reliable results in real-world scenarios.

How long does it take to integrate ImpairmentAI?

Integration typically takes 1-3 days with our SDK, and can be completed in hours with our REST API. Our team provides comprehensive documentation and support throughout the integration process.

What data privacy measures are in place?

We prioritize data privacy with end-to-end encryption, local processing, and anonymous data collection. Our system is fully compliant with GDPR, HIPAA, and other relevant privacy regulations.

Can ImpairmentAI work offline?

Yes, ImpairmentAI can function offline. The core detection system works locally on the device, with optional cloud synchronization when connectivity is available.

What types of impairment can be detected?

Our system can detect various types of impairment including fatigue, intoxication, cognitive distraction, and physical impairment through analysis of multiple behavioral and physiological indicators.
